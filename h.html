PM attends ground-breaking ceremony for new expressway

Prime Minister Nguyen Xuan Phuc attended the ground-breaking ceremony for the construction of the Ha Long-Van Don-Mong Cai expressway in Quang Ninh province on October 16, 2023. The expressway, which is expected to be completed by 2026, will connect the three major cities in the province and boost the economic development of the region.

Vietnam wins gold medal at Asian Games

Vietnam's football team defeated Japan by a score of 2-1 in the final match of the Asian Games on October 15, 2023. This is the first time that Vietnam has won the gold medal in this prestigious tournament.
